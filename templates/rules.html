Rock Paper Scissors: The Rules

Each player picks their weapon from the 3 available:
Rock, Paper, or Scissors.
Each weapon is strong against one, but weak against the remainder of the two. If both players choose the same weapon, it's a tie game. If you wish to play against the computer, select 1 from Number of players. Otherwise, choose your weapon, then pass to the second player to choose theirs.
Pressing the 'START BATTLE!' begins the game To start a new game, press "PLAY GAME"
