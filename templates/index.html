Welcome to the Sport of Kings!

The Rules:

Each player picks their weapon from the 3 available: Rock, Paper, or Scissors. Each weapon is strong against one, but weak against the remainder of the two. If both players choose the same weapon, it's a tie game. If you wish to play against the computer, select 1 from Number of players. Otherwise, choose your weapon, then pass to the second player to choose theirs.
Press 'START BATTLE!' to begin the game
